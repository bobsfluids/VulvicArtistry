Our Vision

FRINGE emerged from a collective desire to create space for feminist abstract avant-garde art that challenges patriarchal norms in the art world. Our gallery celebrates works that deconstruct traditional representations and explore new visual languages.

We believe that abstract art offers powerful opportunities for feminist expressionâ€”allowing artists to move beyond objectification and representational constraints to reveal deeper truths about lived experiences.

Each piece in our collection represents a radical reimagining of form, color, and composition that centers marginalized perspectives and pushes against established artistic boundaries.

Maya Rivero

Jin-Soo's work examines the intersection of gender, technology, and nature through experimental mixed media and unconventional materials.
